FAQ

We want to answer all your questions as quickly and as efficiently as we possibly can. By writing the most frequently asked questions and the answers on this page, it enables us to answer your questions, even before you ask them. That is the aim of this part of our site.

Here are some questions that we get asked regularly. We hope this information will help you in some way.

Q1: Can I hire a car for just a pick up and local drop off?
A1: Yes. We have 1 hour, 2 hour or 4 hour packages for each car.
The one hour is for single pick up and local drop off for just one trip.
2 hour is for multiple trips or for longer distances and a photo session.
4 hour is for a full church wedding. Multiple trips, waiting and photo session.
It all depends on what you require and how many people are going to be passengers.
Q2: Can I view your vehicles before we make a final decision?
A2: Yes, we recommend that you arrange a viewing before making your final decision because we want you to see and feel the car before choosing what is right for you. You cannot really do that unless you are there in front of it.
Q3: Are your weekday wedding transport packages cheaper than a weekend ceremony?
A3: Yes. We offer special weekday discounts for all weddings that take place during the working week. Also, keep an eye out for our special, promotional deals.
Q4: How much does it cost for a four hour session?
A4: Prices start from around £400 for a full church wedding but it all depends on what car you choose, how many people are travelling, the number of trips and the distance covered and also whether it is a weekday or weekend wedding.
