Our blog is the most important part of our website. This is because we can publish stories and articles related to our industry so that our website visitors can get up to date news and information relating to the wedding car hire sector.

Blog

Welcome to our blog. This is where we publish industry relevant news stories on anything wedding or car related. We publish regularly to our blog page so please make sure you come back often to catch up on the latest news and info. You may even wish to write for us and this is always welcomed. If you are an avid blogger and have a unique article which you feel would benefit our visitors, please send it into us via email and our blogging team will take a look at your post. If we like it and if it is not already published elsewhere on the world wide net, we will contact you to accept you as a guest blogger. We only accept posts from bloggers who can commit to a minimum of one article per week. If you love to write and would like to be a part of the virtual blog team, we would love to hear from you today. The most important thing about our blog is that our writers have real passion for the items they write about. An article must be special for the readers to want to come back, time and time again. We hope and trust you find the following articles extremely useful and unique and special, because we would love for you to keep coming back.We also invite guest bloggers to submit articles that they would like us to consider for publication within our blogosphere. If you have a story or news article that you have written and would like to have it shown on our blog, please send it in to us right away. We will give you an answer within 48 hours. As long as the piece is well written (is grammatically correct and has no spelling mistakes in it) and unique, (is not copied or very similar to any other published article on the World Wide Web,) we would normally allow it to go on our blog page.
